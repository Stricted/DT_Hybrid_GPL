Why am I unable to access the Internet on a client?

Procedure

  1. Check that the is powered on.
  2. Check that the SIM/USIM card is working properly.
  3. Check the signal strength and make sure that the is located in an area within wireless network coverage.
  4. Check that the connection between the and the client is established.
  5. Check that the network mode is correct. For details, go to the management page to check .
  6. When accessing the Internet through the , check that parameters such as profile, PPP user name, and password are correct. These parameters are provided by your service provider. For details, go to the management page to check and consult your service provider.
  7. The client is unable to access the Internet if the DHCP service is not activated and the client still needs to obtain a dynamic IP address. In this case, you must assign an IP address to your client.
  8. Check that the network adapter of the client works normally.
  9. Check that the parameters such as , , and of the are correct.
  10. If the problem persists, consult your service provider.
