What should I do if I forget the IP address of the management page?

Procedure

  • Method 1: Launch a web browser and enter http://setup.com to log in to the management page and automatically obtain an IP address.
  • Method 2:
    1. Restore the to factory defaults.
    2. Launch a web browser and enter http://192.168.1.1 to log in to the management page.
